About the Festival

TechVibe Delhi 2025 is India’s most exciting 7-day national tech fest, uniting developers, designers, innovators, and dreamers. With high-energy events across AI, ML, Web Development, Cybersecurity, Hardware, Hackathons, and star-studded concerts, it’s a one-stop tech celebration where code meets creativity. This year, TechVibe Delhi also offers hostel facilities for outstation participants, ensuring a comfortable and immersive experience throughout the fest. Most sessions and events will be hosted by professors and lecturers from renowned colleges across India, adding unmatched credibility and learning value to every track. Further information, including schedules, accommodation details, and guidelines, will be emailed to all participants after successful registration.
